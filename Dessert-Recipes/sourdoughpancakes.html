Sourdough Pancakes

  • 120g milk
  • 15g cornflour
  • 130g flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 120g sourdough starter
  • 20g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 25g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla

  1. Mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda & salt
  2. Separate the egg whites from the yolks
  3. Mix the wet ingredients: milk, butter, yols and vanilla
  4. Mix the egg whites until it becomes foamy, then continue mixing while gradually adding the sugar
  5. Mix the wet, dry & sourdough starter
  6. Finally, fold in the egg whites until incorporated
  7. Cook the pancakes on a medium heat
  8. TIP: Flip the pancakes when bubbles start forming in the center
  9. TIP: Keep the pancakes warm by heating the oven at 90c

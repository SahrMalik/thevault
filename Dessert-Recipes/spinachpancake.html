Spinach Pancake

  • 40g oats
  • 60g milk
  • Handful of spinach
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • A pinch of salt

  1. Place everything in a blender and blend until runny
  2. Cook batter on medium-low heat for 3-4 mins
  3. Flip and cook the other side for a minute

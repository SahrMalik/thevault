Classic Cheesecake

  • 120g digestive biscuits
  • 35g sugar
  • 50g unsalted butter

  • 600g cream cheese
  • 130g sugar
  • 160g sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/2tsp lemon juice
  • 2 large eggs

  1. Preheat oven to 180c
  2. Blend the digestive biscuits, stir in the sugar then pur in the melted butter
  3. Add the crust in a 7 inch cake tin, and pat down gently with your hand or a flat cup
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes
  5. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar on medium speed for 2 minutes until the mixture is creamy
  6. Add the sour cream, vanilla, and lemon juice and beat until fully combined
  7. Add the eggs one at a time, beating after each addition. Stop mixing as soon as the eggs are incorporated
  8. If baking in a cake tin, cover the bottom with foil and place the tin in a tray. Pour 1 inch of boiling water in the tray and bake for 55 minutes
  9. If baking in a cupcake tin, place a small tray at the bottom of the oven and pour in boiling water. Bake for 18 minutes
  10. Let the cheesecake cool at room temp completely then put in the fridge for at least 4 hours

Strawberry Cupcakes

  • 200g caster sugar
  • 200g plain flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 80g milk
  • 80g butter melted
  • 40g vegetable oil
  • 1-2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g strawberries cooked down to 80g (with 1/2 tsp lemon juice)

  1. Preheat oven to 160c fan. Get the cupcake tins ready, this recipe will bake 12 cupcakes
  2. Mix dry ingredients: flour, baking powder & soda, salt
  3. In another bowl, mix the eggs and sugar
  4. Then add the milk, melted butter, oil and vanilla
  5. Stir in the strawberry puree
  6. Fold in dry ingredients until just combined
  7. Bake for 20 - 22 minutes

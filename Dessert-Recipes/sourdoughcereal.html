Sourdough Cinnamon Toast Crunch

  • 110g sourdough starter
  • 60g flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 30g butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar

  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

  1. Mix together the starter, flour, salt, butter & the 2 tbsp sugar
  2. Create a small rectangle block with the dough, then leave it in the fridge for at least 30 mins
  3. Flatten the dough into a large rectangle about a 1/4cm thick
  4. Oil the top of the dough then sprinkle the sugar and cinnamon mix
  5. Cut the dough into small squares using a pizza cutter. Poke holes into each square using a fork
  6. Bake at 180c for around 20 mins, or until the edges start browning

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 280g flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp cornflour

  • 160g butter
  • 100g white sugar
  • 150g light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk
  • 2-3 tsp vanilla
  • 225g mix-ins

  1. Cook the butter until just browned, sift into a bowl and let it cool to room temp
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in a bowl and set aside
  3. Mix the sugars and butter until well combined
  4. Mix in the egg and vanilla into the sugar-butter mix until everything is combined
  5. Sift in the flour and mix until combined
  6. Add the mix-ins
  7. Spoon the cookie dough onto a sheet in the shape of a ball
  8. refrigerate the cookie dough and baking tray for between 2 to 48 hours
  9. Bake the cookies at 180f (fan) for up to 14 minutes

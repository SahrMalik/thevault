Pumpkin Pie

  • Pie crust

  • 400g can pumpkin puree
  • 400g can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp ginger powder
  • 1/8 tsp cloves
  • 1/2 tsp salt

  1. Prepare the pie crust
  2. Mix all the filling ingredients in a bowl
  3. Bake at 180c for 50-70 mins or until the top is brown and the filling is just set
  4. Let it cool and serve with some ice cream or whipped cream

Apple Crumble

  • 5 cooking apples
  • 60g brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp ginger powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 70ml water
  • Handful of dates (optional)

  • 220g flour
  • 160g butter
  • 50g oats
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ginger powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

  1. Peel and chop the apples and place them in water to prevent browning.
  2. Simmer the apples, sugar, spices and water for 10 minutes.
  3. Prepare the crumble by mixing the butter, flour, oats, sugar and spices
  4. Add the apples then the crumble onto a 11" by 6" baking dish
  5. Bake at 180c for 25 minutes or until the crumble is golden brown

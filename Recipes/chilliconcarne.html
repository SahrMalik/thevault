Chilli con carne

  • 500g beef mince (or 400g mushrooms)
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 large or 2 small onions
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch fresh ginger
  • 400ml beef or veggie stock
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 400g canned red kidney beans
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Coriander for garnish

  1. Cook the beef until browned (if using beef) then put it aside
  2. Cook the onions until soft
  3. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add the mushrooms and peppers and cook until soft
  5. Add the tomato paste and spices and cook for 2 minutes
  6. Add the stock and chopped tomatoes and cook for 30-45 minutes
  7. Add the kidney beans 5 minutes before you finish cooking
  8. Add the coriander for garnish and serve with rice

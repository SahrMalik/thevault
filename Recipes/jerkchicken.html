Jerk Chicken

  • 1 small whole chicken (or 1kg - 1.5kg chicken pieces)
  • 4 spring onions
  • 1 onion
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 2 inch piece of ginger
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
  • 1 tsp ground all spice
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cloves
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar

  • 3 tbsp reserved marinade
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp water
  • a squeeze of lemon/lime

  1. Wash the chicken and cut slashes in the thigh and breast
  2. Blend the marinade until it becomes a paste
  3. Make the marinade glaze before adding to chicken
  4. Rub the marinade on the chicken and let it marinade for 4-24 hours
  5. Take chicken out of the fridge 30 minutes before baking
  6. Bake at 220c for 20 minutes then 190c for 40 minutes on a wire rack
  7. During the last 5 or 10 minutes add the marinade glaze on the chicken and turn on the grill/broiler

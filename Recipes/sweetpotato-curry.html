Sweet Potato Curry

  • 2 onions
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 4 fresh thyme springs
  • 1 inch fresh ginger
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2-3 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp all-spice
  • 1/2 tsp coriander
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 150ml vegetable stock
  • 3 medium sweet potatoes
  • 3 cups spinach
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas

  1. Cook onion until soft then add garlic, thyme and ginger. Cook for another minute.
  2. Add in tomatoes and cook until the tomatoes soften.
  3. Add in the tomato paste, sweet potatoes, green peas and spices then cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the coconut milk and stock then cook for 30 mins.
  5. Stir in spinach and cook for 2 minutes.

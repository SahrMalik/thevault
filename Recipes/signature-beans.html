Chickpea curry

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • half an inch fresh ginger
  • 1 small tomato
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1 can of baked beans
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp tomato paste

  1. Fry onions
  2. Add garlic and ginger then fry for a minute
  3. Add tomatoes and mushrooms and fry until soft
  4. Add the tomato paste with spices and continue to cook for another minute
  5. Add the beans and simmer until desired level of thickness
  6. (optional) Squeeze in some fresh lemon juice for added flavour
  7. Serve with toast or as a burrito

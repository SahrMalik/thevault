Shakshuka

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 400g tomato passata or 400g chopped tomatoes
  • sliced peppers
  • spinach (optional)
  • 2-4 eggs
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • pinch of chilli
  • salt & pepper
  • coriander or parsley for garnish

  1. Cook onions on medium heat for 5 minutes
  2. Stir in the garlic and cook for 30 seconds
  3. Add the peppers and spices and cook for 3-5 minutes
  4. Pour in the passata or chopped tomatoes
  5. Let it simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Season with salt & pepper
  7. Stir in the spinach and cook for 2 minutes
  8. Make small wells in the pan and add the eggs in the wells
  9. Cook on medium high for 3 minutes with the lid on
  10. Add herbs and additional salt & pepper on the eggs

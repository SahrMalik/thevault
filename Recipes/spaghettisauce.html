Spaghetti Sauce

  • 2 onions
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried basil (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1-2 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

  1. Cook onions for 6-8 minutes until golden
  2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, oregano, basil, paprika and chilli flakes. Cook for 2 minutes
  4. Pour in chopped tomatoes (+ some watter), sugar, salt, vinegar and black pepper
  5. Simmer for 20 - 40 minutes
  6. Add a small knob of butter at the end
